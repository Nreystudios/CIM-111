Visit Amalfi Coast

Italy 2018

The Amalfi Coast is a 50-kilometer stretch of coastline along the
southern edge of Italy’s Sorrentine Peninsula, in the Campania
region. It’s a popular holiday destination, with sheer cliffs and a
rugged shoreline dotted with small beaches and pastel-colored
fishing villages. The coastal road between the port city of Salerno
and clifftop Sorrento winds past grand villas, terraced
vineyards and cliffside lemon groves.
